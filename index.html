Why DJ Blob?

A fun, simple, and free music bot that allows you to listen to music with your Discord buddies!

Commands

Command Name Description Usage
Play Plays the desired music /play "Music"
Pause Pauses the music /pause
Loop Plays the music on repeat /loop
Leave Leaves the voice channel /leave
Bass Bass boosts the currently playing music /bassboost
Slowed Slows down the currently playing music /slowed
Speed Speeds up the currently playing music /speed
Now Playing Shows the details of the music that's currently playing /nowplaying
Skip Skips the currently playing music to the queued up music /skip
Now Playing Shows the details of the music that's currently playing /nowplaying
Seek Fast forwards music /seek "enter the queue number"
Back Plays the previously played music /back
Volume Sets the volume to your desired level /volume "enter the value"
